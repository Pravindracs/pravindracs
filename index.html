About Me

I am Pravindra Shekhar, a research scholar with a passion for Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. My research focuses on developing intelligent solutions for fault detection in IoT-enabled Wireless Sensor Networks. With over 8 years of research experience, I have contributed significantly to the field through numerous publications in top-tier journals and conferences.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Sensor Networks
  • Mobile Communication
  • Intelligent Fault Detection & Identification
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Machine Learning and AI

Research Experience

  • Senior Researcher at AI Lab, University of Hyderabad (2018 - Present)
  • Research Associate, IoT Lab, Hyderabad (2015 - 2018)
  • Collaborated with international teams on AI-driven IoT solutions for real-time fault detection.

Education
