Biography

Pravindra Shekhar is a research scholar with a focus on machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science. He has over 8 years of experience in conducting research.

He is pursuing Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Hyderabad, where he focused on machine learning algorithms for intelligent fault detection in IoT-enabled WSNs. He has since published numerous papers in top-tier journals and conferences, and his work has been cited widely across various domains.

Pravindra is currently a Senior Researcher at AI Lab, working on Intelligent machine learning approaches for detection of abnormality based of any events.
